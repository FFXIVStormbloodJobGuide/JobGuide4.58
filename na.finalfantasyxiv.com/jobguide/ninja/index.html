Hailing from the war-torn lands of the Far East, the secret arts of the ninja were born of necessity, and have since given rise to a unique breed of highly-trained combatants.

Able to manipulate the vital energies of the land, the air, and living beings, they manifest their power through the weaving of signs, unleashing a wide array of attacks against their foes. Master the arts of the ninja and learn to bend the tide of battle to your will.

Job Gauge

Job gauges are a new user interface element introduced in Patch 4.0. Learn the unique playstyle of each job to truly shine in battle!

Huton Gauge

The Huton Gauge indicates time remaining until the current Huton effect fades.

Ninki Gauge

The Ninki Gauge displays the current amount of Ninki a ninja has accumulated. Ninki slowly builds inside a ninja's body with each auto-attack landed and can be used to execute special ninjutsu such as the Hellfrog Medium (acquired at level 62), Bhavacakra (acquired at level 68), and Ten Chi Jin (acquired at level 70).

Simplified versions of the Huton and Ninki Gauges were introduced in Patch 4.1. Players can enable or disable this UI at any time.

PvP Traits

These traits are common to all jobs, and only take effect when in PvP areas.
The number of PvP traits that can be set is limited to three, so consider your preferred playstyle before making your selections!

Traits

Effect

Increased HP

 Increases maximum HP by 500.

Increased Action Speed

 Decreases weaponskill and spell cast and recast times by 3%.

Increased Damage Dealt

 Increases damage dealt by 3%.

Decreased Damage Taken

 Decreases damage taken by 3%.

Increased Healing Potency

 Increases HP restoration by 5%.

Decreased Recast Time

 Decreases recast time of additional actions by 10%.

Increased Resistance Duration

 Increases duration of resistances by 50%.

Increased MP Regeneration

 Increases MP regeneration rate by 25%.

Increased TP Regeneration

 Increases TP regeneration rate by 40%.

The "Increased Resistance Duration" trait is applied to the following resistances:
PvP: Stun Resistance
PvP: Heavy Resistance
PvP: Bind Resistance
PvP: Slow Resistance
PvP: Silence Resistance
PvP: Sleep Resistance
